Our Heritage

From a Single Stand
to Global Icon.

What began as a small drive-in restaurant in San Bernardino, California has grown into the most recognized brand on the planet. The Golden Arches represent more than food — they stand for community, consistency, and innovation that transcends borders.

Every day, McDonald's welcomes nearly 69 million customers in over 100 countries. From Tokyo to Toronto, São Paulo to Stockholm — the promise remains the same: quality, service, cleanliness, and value.

Our Full Story
1940 Richard & Maurice McDonald open the original restaurant in San Bernardino, CA
1955 Ray Kroc opens the first franchised McDonald's in Des Plaines, IL
1968 The Big Mac is introduced — becoming the world's most iconic burger
2003 "I'm Lovin' It" campaign launches globally — still running today
2026 40,000+ restaurants, leading the industry in digital ordering & sustainability
